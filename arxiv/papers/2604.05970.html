Why it matters

Holographic inverse problems are not just about minimizing loss. They depend on whether the observable set is sufficient to distinguish one geometry from another.

The v20 result shows that:

  • value fit alone can remain ambiguous
  • derivative constraints enforce local structure
  • GEO provides geometric anchoring
  • the minimal identifying set can be smaller than the full probe set
