Why it matters

Holographic inverse problems are not just about training a network until loss goes down. They are about whether the observable set is sufficient to distinguish one geometry from another. This is why EE-only can stay stable yet incomplete, while EE ⊕ WL and then EE ⊕ WL ⊕ GEO move toward identifiable reconstruction.
