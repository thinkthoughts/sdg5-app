Core Paths

  • IA — invalid assignment (inconsistent path)
  • VC — valid construction (consistent path)

Origin Framework

ZOS — Zero-Origin Story

  • reasoning that collapses to or begins from zero
  • permits unconstrained assignment and inconsistency
  • corresponds to IA

GOS — Gravitational-Origin Structure

  • reasoning anchored by constraint-preserving relations
  • resists collapse and accumulates structure
  • corresponds to VC

“gravitational” denotes stability, not a specific physical model

ZABCD (failure modes)

  • Z0 — unconstrained
  • Z1 — collapse
  • Z2 — assignment
  • Z3 — denial
  • Z4 — mixed inconsistency

ABCD (valid construction)

  • A — anchor
  • B — relation
  • C — constraint
  • D — consistency
